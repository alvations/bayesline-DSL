The fourth iteration of the DSL task featuring a multilingual dataset containing excerpts of journalistic texts.

Languages included this year, grouped by similarity are:

- Bosnian (bs), Croatian (hr), and Serbian (sr)
- Malay (my) and Indonesian (id)
- Persian (fa-IR) and Dari (fa-AF)
- Canadian (fr-CA) and Hexagonal French (fr-FR)
- Brazilian (pt-BR) and European Portuguese (pt-PT)
- Argentine (es-AR), Peninsular (es-ES), and Peruvian Spanish (es-PE)

A total of 20k sentences are provided for each of the 14 classes, 18k in a training set and 2k in a development set.
